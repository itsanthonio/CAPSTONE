System Architecture

THE DETECTION PIPELINE.

Our multi-layered approach ensures no environmental disturbance goes undetected — from raw satellite data to actionable field intelligence.

01

Data Acquisition

Ingesting multi-spectral HLS imagery from Landsat-9 and Sentinel-2 constellations. Scenes are cloud-filtered and normalised before inference.

QUERY: hls_assets WHERE cloud_cover < 10
02

Neural Analysis

FPN-ResNet50 CNN identifies patterns of canopy loss, sedimentation, and surface disturbance at 30m resolution across the Ghana mining belt.

EXEC: fpn_resnet50_inference_v1
03

Stakeholder Alert

Verified threats are immediately dispatched to EPA, Forestry Commission, and regional agencies via encrypted notification channels.

STATUS: alert_dispatched_encrypted
